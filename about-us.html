ABOUT US

Many years of experience in providing passengers/luggage rail services means we have developed strong relation with customers, transport bodies and tendering authorities alike. S. Jamil & Co (SJMCPL) is a project of STHN Group; it is the relentless effort o Late Mr.Syed Tasneem Hussain Naqvi (CEO) of STHN Group and his sons. It has Become a leading name in the Pakistan. Their leadership and vigorous skills have taken SJMCPL to one of the leading Rail Operating Company in Pakistan.

SJMCPL has diversified business activities, providing Railway services is one of the core business of STHN Group.

Joint venture with Pakistan railways, SJMCPL operating trains in Pakistan. Our services help to create strong, vibrant and sustainable economies. During the last year around 7 million relied on us to get to work, to college, to visit family and friends and much more and, each day, every one of our above 500 employees works hard to deliver vitally important services for our customers.
